College Application Essay

A college application essay is a vital part of the admissions process, allowing students to present a personal account of who they are beyond academic achievements while demonstrating clear and purposeful communication. It requires knowledge, experience, and authenticity of one being, which are foundational to their college journey.

by Niturada

  • Why they are required
  • Focus and goal of the essay

Parts of the Essay

by Panaligan
  • Introduction: Hook + clear thesis
  • Body: Stories + evidence + school values
  • Conclusion: Synthesize + bold final message

Writing Process

by Igarta
  • Introduction – grab attention with a hook and main ide
  • Body – explain experiences, achievements, or challenges with details
  • Conclusion – reflect on lessons and future goals
  • Be authentic – use your real voice
  • Show, don’t tell – use examples
  • Be clear and concise – avoid unnecessary details

Critiquing Your Essay

by Diaz
  • Definition: A personal essay that reveals your experiences, values, and identity, not just achievements
  • Purpose: Helps readers understand you beyond numbers and academic records
  • Importance: Used by colleges to evaluate character, perspective, and communication skills
  • Core idea: It acts as your written self-introduction; be genuine and reflective

Conclusion

by Ravelo
  • Your essay is you on paper—make it real, not perfect
  • Hook them fast, then let your story carry
  • Real beats impressive every time
  • Start now—your story deserves to be told

Suggestions and Tips

by Felipe
  • Purposeful Writing: Ensuring every paragraph acts as a direct building block.
  • Impactful Phrasing: Breaking repetitive Structures / Varying sentence lengths.
  • Bridge Challenge: Replace generic transitions with full sentences.
  • Key takeaway: Focus on organization and connectivity of ideas & sentences.
